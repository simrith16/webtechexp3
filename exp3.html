About us

The institute is located on a sprawling 720-acre campus, amidst the green vegetation and misty mountains accommodating over 7500 students for learning and Research. The origin of the Karunya Institute of Technology and Sciences dates back to the year 1986 when it began as an Engineering College affiliated with Bharathiar University.
